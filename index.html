My Recipe

A. Ingredients

  • 1 bag of Silky Pudding powder
  • 700 ml of water

B. Cookwares

  • Pot
  • Stove
  • Spoon
  • Serving cups
  • Measuring cup

C. Instructions

  1. Pour the powder into the pot. Then, add 700ml of water
  2. Stir the ingredients together
  3. Switch on the stove. Cook the mixture and stir until boiled
  4. Switch off the stove and serve the mixture into the serving cups
  5. Chill the mixture until room temperature
  6. Put the mixture into the refrigerator and cool for an hour
  7. The pudding is ready to serve
